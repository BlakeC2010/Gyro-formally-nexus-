# Civil Rights Icons

A summary of the primary leaders of the American Civil Rights Movement.

## Martin Luther King Jr.
- **Philosophy:** Nonviolent civil disobedience.
- **Key Actions:** Led the Montgomery bus boycott (1955), "I Have a Dream" speech (1963).
- **Impact:** Instrumental in passing the Civil Rights Act of 1964 and the Voting Rights Act of 1965.

## Rosa Parks
- **Philosophy:** Activism through quiet strength and defiance.
- **Key Actions:** Refused to give up her seat in Montgomery (1955).
- **Impact:** Sparked the year-long Montgomery bus boycott and became the "Mother of the Civil Rights Movement."

## Malcolm X
- **Philosophy:** Black empowerment, self-defense, and human rights.
- **Key Actions:** National spokesperson for the Nation of Islam; later transitioned to global human rights advocacy.
- **Impact:** Pivotal influence on the Black Power movement and African American identity.
